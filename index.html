About Mishin

At Mishin, we specialize in providing end-to-end HR solutions for SMEs and MSMEs. Our focus is to make HR processes seamless, effective, and aligned with your business goals. We believe that the right HR practices can transform organizations, drive growth, and foster a culture of excellence. We have successfully partnered with clients across diverse industries including Information Technology (IT), Infrastructure, Real Estate, Chemicals, Hospitality, Accounting & Finance (e.g., CA firms), Insurance, Jewellery Retail, EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction), and more. Whether you're hiring for entry-level, mid-management, or leadership roles, we support recruitment across all functions and levels as per your business needs.
